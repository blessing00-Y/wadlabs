I interviewed the co-owner of "Grab a Bite," a local restaurant she started with her husband to generate income ahead of their retirement.
The purpose of the webpage is to showcase their authentic menu and direct potential customers to their primary platform.
The target audience is local diners looking for fresh African dishes and fast food. The owner preferred a red color scheme to match their physical storefront and attract attention.
Because they do not have specific restaurant contact channels and fulfill orders exclusively through Glovo, the website utilizes a clean, straightforward design that highlights their menu pricing, location, and third-party delivery access.
